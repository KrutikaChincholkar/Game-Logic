📖 HOW TO PLAY
✕
🎯 Objective
Score the most points after 13 turns. Each turn you roll 5 dice up to 3 times, then claim one combination on the scorecard. Highest total wins!
🎲 Rolling Dice
1. Tap ROLL to roll all 5 dice (3 rolls per turn).
2. Tap any die to lock it — locked dice won't re-roll.
3. Tap again to unlock.
4. After rolling, tap a row to select your score, then tap PLAY.

⭐ 5 of a Kind Bonus
Roll a 5X after scoring it for 50? Earn +100 bonus! Must score immediately. The 5X acts as a Joker — use it for FH (25), SM (30), or LG (40).

💡 Tips
🎯 Aim for 63+ in Upper for the +35 bonus — often decides the winner.

🎲 Save Chance for turns where nothing else scores.

🔒 Lock good dice early, re-roll the rest.

🤔 Sometimes scoring 0 in a weak slot beats wasting a good roll.
